The Name

Named for the goddess who out-thought Zeus.

In Greek myth, Metis was the Titan of wisdom and cunning counsel — the mind the King of the Gods feared enough to swallow whole, rather than risk what she might create next.

We kept her name and gave it a modern accent: Metisova. It isn't a metaphor picked to dress up a logo — it's the operating model. Think first. Invent second. Build third, and never skip a step to move faster.

Metisova is the parent company. Everything beneath it — the labs, the models, the research — answers to that same discipline, whichever field the problem happens to live in.

The Metis Principle

Three rules every division follows.

Different fields, same discipline. Every division inside Metisova is held to the same three commitments before anything ships.

⚡

Original invention first

We don't clone existing tools or ship incremental features. Every product starts from first principles, aimed at a capability that didn't exist before.

🌍

Grounded in the real world

Our work is anchored to real people, real places, and real outcomes — not abstractions. If it doesn't hold up outside a demo, it isn't finished.

🧭

Safety before scale

Wherever a product touches real people's data, location, or trust, the safeguards are part of the first design — never a patch added after launch.

How We Work

Not a funnel. A loop.

Most companies pick a problem once. Metisova keeps cycling through the same three moves, in every field it enters, indefinitely.

Identify

We look for problems people have quietly learned to route around — treated as permanent facts of life rather than unsolved engineering questions.

Invent

We build something original to solve it — not a repackaging of an existing tool, but a genuine invention, tested until it actually holds up.

Commercialize

We turn the invention into a product or service real people can use and real businesses can run — funding the next loop.

Strategic Roadmap

From first invention to global scale.

Phase I — Foundation
Flagship prototypes & safety architecture
Build working prototypes of the live translator and the Atlas MVP, with the friend/stranger safety model designed in from the first line of code — not added after launch.
Phase II — Expansion
Regional launch & model release
Launch the Nigerian-language LLM and the AI tutor, and take Atlas live in a small number of dense, well-understood markets before expanding further.
Phase III — Scale
Bioscience commercialization & global reach
Commercialize the low-cost sequencing method and extend the Metisova ecosystem toward the "genomic profile for everyone, everywhere" goal.
Who This Is For

Built to be read two ways.

For investors

Metisova is structured as a holding company running multiple deep-tech bets under one operating discipline, rather than a single product bet.

  • One thesis — identify, invent, commercialize — applied consistently across every division.
  • Diversified across software, applied AI, bioscience, and consumer platforms, reducing single-market risk.

  • Distance shouldn't be the only reason you never got to see a place, or meet the people in it.
